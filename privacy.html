Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Urban Freight Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, submit a form, or communicate with us by phone, email, or SMS.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect contact information, business/application information, website usage information, and communication information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to review applications, provide freight and logistics services, send load updates and confirmations, respond to inquiries, maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable laws.

3. SMS and Text Messaging

When you provide your mobile number and agree to receive text messages, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your account, loads, updates, verifications, and service-related communications. Text STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers, shippers, brokers, business partners, government authorities when required, or other parties with your consent. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for business purposes, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us to update information, opt out of marketing emails, or opt out of SMS messages by texting STOP.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and revise the effective date above.
