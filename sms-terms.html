Urban Freight Inc. (“Urban Freight,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates one or more SMS text message programs to communicate with carriers, drivers, contractors, and customers. These messages may include:

Program Name: Urban Freight Alerts

2. How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from Urban Freight when you:

  • Provide your mobile number on an Urban Freight web form and indicate that you agree to receive text messages; or
  • Provide your mobile number to Urban Freight staff (for example, by phone, email, or in person) and confirm that you agree to receive text messages.

By opting in, you confirm that you are the account holder or regular user of the mobile number provided and that you are authorized to receive SMS messages at that number.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency will vary based on your relationship with Urban Freight and the number of active loads or requests. You may receive multiple messages per week when actively working with us.

4. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of the SMS program at any time by texting STOP in reply to any message you receive from us. After you send STOP, we will send one final confirmation message stating that you have been unsubscribed, and you will not receive additional SMS messages unless you opt in again.
