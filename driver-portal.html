Drivers · Urban Freight Inc.

Upload BOLs and request pay in two simple steps.

1) Use the left form when you are loading at the shipper.
2) Use the right form when you are delivered and ready for driver pay.

Always include: Driver name, Truck #, Trailer #, Load #, and clear photos of every page.

Pickup · Before leaving shipper

Pickup BOL / Loading Photos

Use this while you are being loaded or right after loading. Attach pictures of the BOL, pallets, and trailer before you leave the shipper.

You can select multiple photos at once. If you use the camera, take one photo, tap “Use photo”, then tap this box again to add more photos.

Pickup photos go straight to Urban Freight dispatch.

Delivery · After POD signed + ready for pay

Delivery POD / BOL + Driver Pay

Use this after delivery when the POD/BOL is signed and you are ready to request pay, settlement review, or an advance.

Attach clear photos of every page plus lumper / detention receipts. You can add multiple photos the same way as on the pickup form.

Delivery documents and pay requests go straight to accounting/dispatch.
