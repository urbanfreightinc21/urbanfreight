SMS Program

SMS Terms & Conditions

Terms for Urban Freight Alerts and operational text messages related to loads, onboarding, appointments, and account communication.

Effective Date: November 19, 2025

1. Program Description

Urban Freight Inc. operates SMS text message programs to communicate with carriers, drivers, contractors, and customers. Messages may include load offers, updates, confirmations, check calls, appointment reminders, onboarding, credential/account notifications, service updates, alerts, and important notices.

Program Name: Urban Freight Alerts

2. How to Opt In

You may opt in when you provide your mobile number on an Urban Freight web form and indicate consent to receive text messages, or when you provide your number to Urban Freight staff and confirm consent.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your relationship with Urban Freight and active loads or requests. You may receive multiple messages per week when actively working with us.

4. How to Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by texting STOP in reply to any message. After STOP, we will send one final confirmation message and you will not receive additional SMS messages unless you opt in again.

5. Help and Support

For help, text HELP in reply to any message or contact us:

6. Carrier Charges

Message and data rates may apply according to your wireless plan. Urban Freight does not charge a separate fee for sending SMS messages.

7. Supported Carriers

Our SMS services are supported by major U.S. wireless carriers. Participation may not be available on all carriers or in all areas, and delivery is subject to your carrier and network operator.

8. Privacy

Information collected through our SMS program is used to provide services, manage your account, and communicate about loads and related matters. Review our Privacy Policy.

9. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old or the age of majority in your jurisdiction and a resident of the United States to participate.
