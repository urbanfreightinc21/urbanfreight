SMS Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: November 19, 2025

Urban Freight Inc. operates SMS text message programs to communicate with drivers, contractors, carriers, and customers about loads, onboarding, appointments, and account updates.

1. Program Description

Messages may include load offers, updates, confirmations, check calls, appointment reminders, onboarding notices, service updates, and important alerts. Program Name: Urban Freight Alerts.

2. How to Opt In

You may opt in by providing your mobile number on an Urban Freight web form and indicating agreement to receive text messages, or by providing your number to Urban Freight staff and confirming consent.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your relationship with Urban Freight and active loads or requests.

4. How to Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by texting STOP in reply to any message. After STOP, you will receive one final confirmation and no additional SMS messages unless you opt in again.

5. Help & Support

For help, text HELP in reply to a message, email dispatch@urbanfreightinc.com, or call (559) 825-9890.

6. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply according to your wireless service plan. Urban Freight does not charge a separate fee for SMS messages.

7. Privacy

Information collected through SMS is used to provide services, manage accounts, and communicate about loads and related matters. See our Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to These Terms

We may update these SMS Terms & Conditions occasionally and revise the effective date above.
