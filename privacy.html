Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Urban Freight Inc ("Urban Freight", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website and when you interact with us, including through phone, email, and SMS.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information such as name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Business information such as company name, MC/DOT number, and billing details.
  • Website usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed.
  • Communication data such as emails, call logs, and SMS messages exchanged with us.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide trucking and freight brokerage services.
  • Communicate with shippers, brokers, and carriers about loads and operations.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Verify identity and protect against fraud or misuse.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. SMS Communications

Urban Freight primarily uses SMS to receive one-time verification codes and security alerts from brokers, load boards, factoring companies, and other transportation partners. We do not send marketing or promotional SMS messages to the general public.

If we ever send you SMS messages directly (for example, operational updates or account notifications), you may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, you may reply HELP or contact us using the information below. Message and data rates may apply.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information:

  • With brokers, shippers, and carriers as needed to perform transportation services.
  • With vendors and service providers (such as phone and software providers) who work on our behalf.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure, and you use our website and services at your own risk.

6. Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request to update or correct your contact information.
  • You may opt out of SMS where applicable by replying STOP.
  • You may contact us to inquire about what personal information we hold about you.
